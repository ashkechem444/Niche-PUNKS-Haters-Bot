# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [06/28/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how "Niche PUNKS Haters" (referred to as "we", "our", or "the Bot") collects, uses, and protects user data.

## 1. What Data We Collect
To provide core functionality, this Bot collects and stores the following:
*   **Discord User ID**: Used to uniquely identify your profile.
*   **Discord Username**: Used to display your name in lists and tables.
*   **User-Submitted Game Data**: The class, helmet, armor, and weapon stats you provide via the `/sheet` command.

## 2. How We Use This Data
The collected data is used exclusively to:
*   Respond to your commands and save your gear setup.
*   Display the saved data to other users via commands like `/table` and `/classlist`.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
All collected data is stored locally in a CSV file (`MasterSheet.csv`) on the server hosting the Bot. We take reasonable measures to protect this data; however, no electronic storage method is 100% secure.

## 4. Do We Share Your Data?
**We do not** sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of your stored data at any time. Please contact the owner ashkechem444 to make a request.

## 6. Policy Updates
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of significant changes in our support server.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the owner ashkechem444.
